# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NimHub serves three primary user groups:
- **Nimiq community members**: Managing their NIM holdings and purchasing goods/services
- **Everyday crypto users**: Simple payments and gift cards via conversational UI
- **Small merchants**: Accepting NIM payments with QR codes
All users expect mobile‑first, conversational (text and voice) interactions with the AI assistant.

## Product Purpose

NimHub is an AI‑powered payment platform for Nimiq cryptocurrency. It enables users to send NIM, buy gift cards, top up airtime, and pay bills via natural language (text or voice), with QR code support for easy sharing. Success means users feel secure, the interface is simple to use, and payments complete without friction.

## Brand Personality

- **Secure**: Enterprise‑grade security is the foundation of trust
- **Simple**: Intuitive conversational and visual interactions, no crypto jargon
- **Friendly**: Approachable tone, warm design, and helpful AI guidance

## Anti-references

NimHub should NOT look like:
- **Generic crypto exchanges**: No cluttered trading views or overwhelming charts
- **Skeuomorphic payment apps**: No old‑fashioned, flashy design elements
- **Minimalist but unusable interfaces**: No designs that trade clarity for too‑simple aesthetics

## Design Principles

1. **Conversational first**: Lead with chat/voice interactions, keep text natural
2. **Trust through clarity**: Security features are visible but not intimidating
3. **Mobile native**: Designed for phones first, tablet/desktop second
4. **Crypto without jargon**: Explain terms simply, show NIM values alongside real‑world currency
5. **Frictionless payments**: Minimize steps between intent and completed transaction

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Reduced‑motion support for users who prefer it
- Color‑blind friendly design (no information conveyed by color alone)
- Mobile‑first responsive layout optimized for all screen sizes
